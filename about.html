Rino Efendić was born in 1961 in Sinj. He graduated from the School of Fine Arts in Split. From 1981 to 1983 he studied camera at the Academy of Film, Theater and Television in Zagreb. From 1983 to 1988 he studied art history and ethnology at the Faculty of Philosophy in Zagreb. He worked at the Design Studio "BEPO Zenit" in Sinj (1988-1990) and taught at the School of Fine Arts in Split (1990-1995). He has been running the Small School of Photography at OPUS and the Otok Club, Dubrovnik and nowdays he holds lectures in Kino klub Split. One of co-founders of Kvart - Association for Contemporary Art. The aim of the association is to promote contemporary art as an opportunity to reflect and comment on their own reality to the local population, with the ultimate aspiration to involve them in similar actions.
